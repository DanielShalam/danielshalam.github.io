Research Summary

I develop methods for representation learning and multimodal alignment with a focus on flow-based and training-efficient objectives. My Ph.D. work proposes Few-Shot Flow (FSF) - aligning independent vision and text encoders for few-shot classification without paired training data (orthogonal Procrustes alignment + flow-matching prior).
